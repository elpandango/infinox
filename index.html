WHAT IS FOREX?

Forex as in industry has a reported over $5 trillion a day turnover, with trading in many locations and exchanges 24 hours a day from Sunday night through to Friday night. Foreign exchange trading enables you to take advantage of rate fluctuations across a wide range of currencies. Forex pricing is based on the price relationship between two currencies, such as GBP/USD. Each such relationship is called a pair. You speculate on whether the price of one country's currency will rise or fall against the currency of another country, and take a position accordingly.

RANGE FUTURES – NEW EXCITING FINANCIAL INSTRUMENT

INFINOX CUBOID is a new foreign exchange instrument. Early mover advantage for companies that are appropriately regulated. Opens a new category. Alongside CFDs, FOREX, binary options and share trading.

Invest in an underlying asset by setting a price range (max-min) in the future (start-stop).

If the value of the asset is within the price range during you future time period, you make a full return on your investment.

If the asset price is only within the price range for a part of the future time, you pro rata make a smaller return.

If the asset price never is within the price range, you make no return - but you cannot lose more than you invest.
